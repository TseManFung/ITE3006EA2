Abstract

Lycoris Recoil (Japanese: リコリス・リコイル, Hepburn: Rikorisu・Rikoiru) is a Japanese original anime television series created by Spider Lily and Asaura.

It was produced by A-1 Pictures and aired from July to September 2022.

Set in Tokyo, Lycoris Recoil follows two young orphaned teenage girls named Chisato Nishikigi and Takina Inoue.
